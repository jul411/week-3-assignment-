Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how SimpleTax handles your data.

Data Collection

SimpleTax does not collect, transmit, or sell any personal data. All information you enter stays on your device using local browser storage (localStorage).

Use of Information

Any information you input into the site is used solely for the purpose of helping you complete tax forms. We do not access or use your data for any other reason.

Local Storage

Your entries are saved locally in your browser. Clearing your browser data may erase your stored information. We recommend backing up any important data externally.

Third-Party Access

We do not share your data with third parties. There are no trackers or external analytics services embedded in our platform.

Security

While we do not store data on our servers, we encourage you to use secure devices and networks when using SimpleTax.

Policy Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Please check this page periodically for updates.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at simpletax@example.com.
