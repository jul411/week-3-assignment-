Helpful Tax Resources

The following links provide access to official tax-related tools, forms, and information. These are publicly available and maintained by government agencies.

Federal Resources

  • IRS Main Website – Get general tax information and updates.
  • IRS Forms & Instructions – Download and view tax forms like 1040, W-2, and more.
  • Check Your Refund Status – Track the status of your federal tax refund.
  • Find Your Local IRS Office – Schedule appointments or get walk-in assistance.
  • Interactive Tax Assistant – Use guided tools to answer tax questions.
  • Tax Withholding Estimator – Estimate how much tax should be withheld from your paycheck.

State & Financial Resources

  • State Tax Agencies – Links to all 50 state tax websites.
  • MyMoney.gov – U.S. government site for financial literacy and money management.
  • Managing Your Money – Consumer.gov – Learn basic financial habits and planning tips.
